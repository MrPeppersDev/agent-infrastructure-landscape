Comprehensive list of memory layers, framework-embedded memory, platform features, research systems, retrieval-as-memory hybrids, file-backed paradigms, knowledge-graph platforms, vector-DB infrastructure, and enterprise-search adjacencies. The largest section is at the bottom — recent method papers without distinct products, organised by memory category. Sourced from arxiv.org/abs/2512.13564 (Memory in the Age of AI Agents survey), the Agent-Memory-Paper-List, Awesome-GraphMemory, and the LongMemEval / LoCoMo / ConvoMem leaderboards.

Dedicated memory layers
Standalone services whose entire product is memory for AI agents. They sit between your agent code and durable storage — handling extraction, governance, contradiction resolution, and recall via API. The "database vendor" analogue for the memory tier: you bring an agent, they bring durability.
