## Surprises

- **OpenAI Operator's headline benchmarks** (Jan 2025): OSWorld
  38.1%, WebArena 58.1%, WebVoyager 87% — significantly ahead of
  Anthropic Computer Use's published numbers, but Operator runs in
  OpenAI's sandboxed browser (advantage). Project Mariner's
  WebVoyager 83.5% (Dec 2024) is the closest comparable.
- **Browser Use at 60k+ stars** — the single most-starred AI agent
  OSS project of 2025; eclipsed Auto-GPT's mindshare; $17M seed from
  Felicis. ETH Zurich-founded (Magnus Müller + Gregor Žunič).
- **Cartesia Sonic at ~40ms TTS TTFB** is the lowest published in
  the voice-platform space; founder Albert Gu is the Mamba (SSM)
  author. This is the first commercial validation of SSMs at scale.
- **LiveKit chosen by OpenAI as the WebRTC backbone for Realtime
  API** — an OSS Apache 2.0 framework now powering the most
  high-profile voice-agent API. LiveKit's $45M Series B (Altimeter,
  Sep-2024) is partly a bet on this distribution.
- **Covariant Amazon acqui-hire (Aug 2024)** — first major
  robotics-FM acquisition; Pieter Abbeel + Peter Chen + Rocky Duan +
  Tianhao Zhang all to Amazon Robotics; Covariant remains operating
  under license. Reported $1.5B-equivalent value.
- **Physical Intelligence raised $400M Series A** less than 12
  months after founding (Mar 2024 → Nov 2024) at $2.4B val; π0
  paper published the month before the round. Bezos lead investor,
  with OpenAI + Thrive + Lux participating.
- **Figure AI reportedly at $39.5B val Q2-2025**, up from $2.6B
  Feb-2024 — fastest valuation ramp in humanoids; rumour-stage
  numbers but reported by multiple outlets.
- **NVIDIA GR00T N1 open weights** (Mar 2025, NVIDIA Open Model
  license) is the first open-weights humanoid FM from a major
  silicon vendor — substantial difference from Figure's closed
  Helix or DeepMind's closed Gemini Robotics.
- **TRI LBM diffusion-policy line has 5000+ citations** (Chi et al.
  2023, arXiv 2303.04137) — single most-cited robotics-FM paper of
  the 2023-2025 wave; foundational for many subsequent works
  (including π0).
- **Agility Robotics' Digit in production at GXO since Sep 2024** is
  arguably the only US humanoid currently doing paid work — most of
  the headline-grabbing humanoid programs (Figure, 1X, Apptronik,
  Sanctuary) are still pilot-stage.
