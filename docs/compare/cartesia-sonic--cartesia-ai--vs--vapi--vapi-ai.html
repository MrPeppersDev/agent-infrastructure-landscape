Pros & cons

Cartesia Sonic

Pros: Lowest-latency TTS in market; Mamba-author founders; $64M Series A; SOC 2.

Cons: TTS-only at platform layer — needs orchestrator; SSM voice quality polarising vs Eleven.

Vapi

Pros: Category-leading mindshare; Bessemer-backed; SOC 2 + HIPAA; mature webhooks + SDKs.

Cons: Per-minute economics at scale; provider-passthrough means triple vendor exposure (STT + LLM + TTS).
