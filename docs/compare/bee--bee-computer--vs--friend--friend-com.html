Pros & cons

Bee

Pros: Never stores raw audio — derived-text-only architecture is the strongest privacy model in the wearable category; Amazon acquisition gives runway.

Cons: Cloud-only processing; depends on Amazon's roadmap post-acquisition.

Friend

Pros: Companionship framing rather than productivity — addresses a different need than the rest of the wearable category.

Cons: Consumer reaction has been mixed; cloud LLM backend with limited privacy disclosure.
