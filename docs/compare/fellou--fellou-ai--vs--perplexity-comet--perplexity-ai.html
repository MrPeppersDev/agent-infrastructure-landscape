Pros & cons

Fellou

Pros: Agentic browser with first-class memory of past task trajectories, not just URLs visited.

Cons: New entrant with limited track record; sustainability depends on funding traction.

Perplexity Comet

Pros: First search-engine-native browser agent — memory of past searches and tabs is part of the product premise.

Cons: Limited to what Perplexity sees; cross-browser portability is not on the roadmap.
