Pros & cons

Craft AI

Pros: Document-style structured AI works inside Craft's writing surface rather than as a chat overlay.

Cons: Craft's overall PKM market share has shrunk; AI is an enhancement layer, not a memory product.

Microsoft Recall

Pros: Most aggressive ambient capture of any major OS — every screen seen is searchable, then queryable.

Cons: Drew significant privacy backlash on launch (May 2024); only Copilot+ PC hardware supported.
