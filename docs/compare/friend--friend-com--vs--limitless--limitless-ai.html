Pros & cons

Friend

Pros: Companionship framing rather than productivity — addresses a different need than the rest of the wearable category.

Cons: Consumer reaction has been mixed; cloud LLM backend with limited privacy disclosure.

Limitless

Pros: Local-first lifelog architecture (originated as Rewind) is privacy-stronger than competitors; Meta acquisition gives it deep funding.

Cons: Recent platform pivot from Rewind macOS; product direction post-acquisition uncertain.
