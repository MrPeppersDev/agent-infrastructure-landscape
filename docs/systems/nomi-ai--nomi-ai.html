Nomi AI

https://nomi.ai

AI companion built around long-term memory as primary differentiator. Mind Map 2.0 gives users a navigable graph of what their Nomi knows about them and how memories connect — the most transparent memory model in the companion segment. Users can edit/delete nodes.
