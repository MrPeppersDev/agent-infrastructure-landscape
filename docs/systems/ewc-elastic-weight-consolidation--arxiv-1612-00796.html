Pros & cons

Pros

Kirkpatrick et al. — foundational PNAS 2017 paper; analogous to synaptic consolidation in biology.

Cons

Best on small task sequences; degrades when task count grows; LLM-scale applicability limited.

Claims & capabilities

Foundational continual-learning method analogous to synaptic consolidation; selectively slows learning on weights important for prior tasks. Headline: enables a single network to learn many tasks sequentially without forgetting (no specific quantitative headline in abstract); baseline: SGD without consolidation; primary datasets: permuted-MNIST and several Atari 2600 games.
