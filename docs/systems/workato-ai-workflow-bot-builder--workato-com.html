Workato AI (Workflow Bot Builder)

https://www.workato.com/ai

Workato is the enterprise iPaaS (integration platform) leader — $200M+ ARR, $5.7B valuation 2021. 2023-24 launched Workato AI / Workbot AI with AI-agent workflow sandbox capability inside its automation runtime. Used by Slack-bot / automation customers for AI-augmented workflows. Distinct from pure agent frameworks (CrewAI / LangGraph) by being deeply enterprise-iPaaS-anchored.

  • Daytona T2

  • e2b T1

Apache 2.0 OSS + managed cloud. $13M Seed + Series A 2024 (Sunflower Capital + a16z).
