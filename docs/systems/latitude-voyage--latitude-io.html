Latitude / Voyage

https://latitude.io

AI Dungeon's maker. Voyage (April 2026) launched as a creator platform for AI RPGs. World Engine (5 years in dev) is dedicated AI tracking characters, objects, backstories, relationships independently from the narrative AI; persistent world state evolves with player choices.
