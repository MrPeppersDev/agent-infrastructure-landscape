Claims & capabilities

Web agents self-improve by discovering and synthesizing reusable skills as APIs; agents explore websites, practice skills, distill experiences into robust APIs; 31.8% relative success-rate improvement on WebArena; 39.8% improvement on real-world websites; up to 54.3% enhancement when stronger agents APIs transfer to weaker ones
