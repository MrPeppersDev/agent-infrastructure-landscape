Plaud NotePin

https://www.plaud.ai

Magnetic clip-on wearable. Records in-person conversations and calls; AI summaries, action items, searchable transcripts. AES-256 + TLS 1.2 in transit; doesn't retain audio after transcription. Also sells Note (card-form recorder) and Note Pro.
