HeLa-Mem

https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.16839

Co-activation strengthens associative edges (Hebbian Distillation); separate consolidation pass periodically extracts semantic summaries from the episodic graph — directly modelling slow-wave hippocampal-to-cortical consolidation. Dual-path retrieval over both stores.
