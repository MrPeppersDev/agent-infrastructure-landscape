Sema4.ai

https://sema4.ai/

Sema4.ai is the AI-agent rebrand of Robocorp (RPA / Python automation OSS). Funded by Benchmark, Canvas Ventures, Slow Ventures. Founded 2019 (as Robocorp); rebranded 2024. Positioned as enterprise Python-based agent runtime with strong RPA heritage and governance focus. OSS Robocorp framework remains open-source (Apache 2.0). Distinct from no-code RPA — Sema4.ai is code-first.
