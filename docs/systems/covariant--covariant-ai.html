Covariant

https://covariant.ai/

Pieter Abbeel / Peter Chen-founded robotics FM company — built RFM-1 (Robotics Foundation Model, Mar 2024) for warehouse pick-and-place. Acqui-hired by Amazon Aug-2024 (founders + 25% of team to Amazon Robotics); Covariant remains operating as licensee. The first major robotics-FM acquisition.
