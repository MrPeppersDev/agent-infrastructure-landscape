Pros & cons

Pros

Most established meeting-bot brand with wide Zoom/Meet/Teams coverage and decent enterprise admin controls.

Cons

Memory is meeting-archive style — searchable transcripts but no semantic threading across meetings; limited multilingual coverage.

Free 300 min/mo, Pro $10/user/mo (annual), Business $20/user/mo. Real-time transcription in English, French, Spanish.
