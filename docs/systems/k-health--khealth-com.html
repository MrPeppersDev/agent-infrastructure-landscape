K Health

https://khealth.com/

Primary care / virtual physician intake. Two-agent pipeline: one extracts the relevant EMR subset, second answers grounded to that filtered history. Architecture explicitly diverges by patient condition (diabetic vs heart-failure get different responses to same intake).

When to use

Optimised for: HIPAA compliance + clinical-grade provenance + EHR integration

Anti-fit: not for non-healthcare verticals; must operate under HIPAA / regional health regulation

Pros & cons

Pros

Long-running clinical AI with memory grounded in millions of de-identified patient-symptom trajectories — actual ground-truth corpus.

Cons

K Health's data is proprietary — memory benefits don't transfer to other products; primary care scope.

Claims & capabilities

Matched physician decisions in 2/3 of real cases. Fine-tuned on Gemma 3 via Vertex AI. Live on Mayo Clinic Platform (May 2025); Northwell partnership; HBS case study.
