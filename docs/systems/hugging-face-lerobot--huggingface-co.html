Hugging Face LeRobot

https://huggingface.co/lerobot

Hugging Face's open-source robotics platform — datasets, baselines, training code for imitation learning + diffusion policy + ACT. Launched May 2024; growing rapidly with community contributions (HopeJR, SO-100 arms). LeRobot is the de-facto OSS robotics-ML platform analogous to transformers.
