Claims & capabilities

Human-demonstration-based mobile GUI agent with DemoParser/KnowSeeker/ActExecutor; LearnGUI dataset. Headline: Gemini-1.5-Pro 19.3%->51.7% with single demo (+198.9% relative); UI-TARS-7B-SFT 18.1%->32.8% online (+14.7%); baselines: zero-shot inference of same models; SPHINX-GUI Agent (67.2% on AMEX); primary datasets: LearnGUI-Offline (2,252 tasks across 9 apps) and LearnGUI-Online (101 tasks across 20 apps).
