Pros & cons

Pros

Defined recurrent memory tokens in transformer attention — foundational for segment-recurrent architectures.

Cons

Training instability at scale; superseded for production by attention-window techniques.

Claims & capabilities

Scaling variant (arxiv 2304.11062) handles up to 2M-token sequences with high retrieval accuracy. On par with Transformer-XL for small memory; outperforms it for long-sequence tasks.
