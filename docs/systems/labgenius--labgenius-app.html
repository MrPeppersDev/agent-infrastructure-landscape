LabGenius

https://www.labgenius.app/

Therapeutic-antibody discovery in a closed Design-Build-Test-Learn loop. EVA accumulates structured outcome data from each robotic experiment cycle and feeds it back to generative ML models directing the next design round. Genuine experiment-memory-as-training-signal.
